FN Amplifications

My first ever Slimefun addon, it features a lof of things from power generators, machineries, block breakers, staffs and many more! It was first released back in november 2021 as an personal addon for a server called BarangayCraft and I further developed it to become an official community addon for slimefun. It was rewritten this february and march to improve code maintainabilty and readability. I have added a lot of things since then and will continute to further develop it. Boomer has also covered the addon when it was released last november.



Since then a lot has changed and updates keep coming that gets featured in boomers weekly slimefun updates.

Relics of Cthonia

My entry for slimefun addon jam 2022 which started from june 3-13, it introduces cthonian relics in the nether biome for which an adventurer can unearthed or be obtained from dark mobs. These relics can be traded to piglins where one can receive awesome rewards!



There are 7 common, uncommon and rare relics while 6 relics for epic and legendary.


This plugin is high configurable in terms of drop rates, where do the relics drop (blocks or in mobs) and the amount of piglin rewards you receive from a successfull barter per each relic. You can add additional item drop rewards or where to drop relic in the fields or remove them at your own disposal. Item rewards support slimefun and vanilla items using the appropriate item ID.
You can also generate random config templates by deleting the relic-settings.yml file.
